List of commands:

Join channel commands (only works in "botbear1110" and "hotbear1110"): Description:
bb channel join The bot joins your channel
Notify commands: Description:
bb notify Gives a list of available notify events
bb notify live Adds you to the live notify
bb notify title Adds you to the title notify
bb notify game Adds you to the game notify
bb remove Gives a list of available remove events
bb remove live Removes you from the live notify
bb remove title Removes you from the title notify
bb remove game Removes you from the title game
Other commands: Description:
bb commands Gives a list of commands
bb bot A short explanation of the bot
